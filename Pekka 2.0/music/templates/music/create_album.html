What is Pekka 2.0?

Viberr is a website dedicated to students with questions, and a homage to a certain professor. Here, students, lecturers may participate in various discussions, and vote for them.

Tips for questions

  • Try to be specific.
  • Put the details in the text, not the title
  • Square images look best

Tips for answering

  • Try to visualize or make intuitive explanations.
  • Summaries are great.

Overall participation

  • Give credit to good questions and answers.
  • Refrain from offtopic discussions.
  • Be civil.
